About

Find Out More About Us

Trans Gulf Capitalizes on our 12 year history as a transportation leader to provide our customers with a customized transportation solution anywhere throughout the middle east.

We offers trailers, flat bed, reefer and to a new set of products such as, trans-loading services, shipment for local as well as for the entire Middle East. Transgulf creates model flexibility to help our customers bring their product to market. If you need it moved, transgulf has the product for you.

Transgulf Trading & Logistics Company is one of the few companies serving a variety of companies from diverse industry groups, companies who are themselves leaders in their respective industries.

The professionals at Transgulf have been hard at work for nearly 12 years, so if you need solutions consider it done.
